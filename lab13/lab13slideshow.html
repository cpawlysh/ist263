Planets Slideshow

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun in the Solar System.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in size and composition to Earth.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the only known celestial body to support life.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System and is known for its prominent bands of clouds and the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is known for its stunning ring system, which is made up of ice particles, rocks, and dust.

Uranus

Uranus is a gas giant and is unique among the planets because it rotates on its side.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun in the Solar System.
